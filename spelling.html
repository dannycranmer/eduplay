🐝 Spelling Bee

See the word, memorize it, spell it from memory!

✅ 0 ❌ 0 🔥 0

Choose a difficulty level to start!

A word will appear on screen for 2 seconds. Memorize it, then spell it from memory! 10 words per round.

For Parents & Teachers

This spelling bee game builds visual memory and spelling accuracy. Children see a word briefly, then must recall and type it correctly — strengthening the connection between seeing and spelling.

  • Easy (K-1): 30 high-frequency Dolch sight words (the, and, cat, dog, etc.)
  • Medium (2-3): 30 words for developing readers (because, friend, people, etc.)
  • Hard (4-6): 30 challenging words (beautiful, necessary, environment, etc.)
  • Words display for 2 seconds with a countdown before the spelling prompt
  • Letter-by-letter feedback highlights exactly which letters were correct or wrong
  • No data is saved or sent anywhere
