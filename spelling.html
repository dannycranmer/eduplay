For Parents & Teachers

This spelling game builds word recognition and spelling accuracy through visual associations. Children see an emoji picture and type the corresponding word.

  • Easy: Common 3-4 letter words (cat, dog, sun, cup)
  • Medium: 5-6 letter words (horse, grape, cloud, heart)
  • Hard: 7+ letter words (elephant, butterfly, umbrella)
  • Letter blanks show word length as a visual scaffold
  • "Show Hint" reveals the first letter if stuck
  • No data is saved or sent anywhere
