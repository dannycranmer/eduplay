For Parents & Teachers

Sight words (also called high-frequency words) are the most common words in English text. Recognizing them instantly helps children read fluently without sounding out every word.

  • Pre-K: 20 basic words (I, a, the, is, it, in, etc.)
  • Kindergarten: Dolch Kindergarten list (all, am, are, at, ate, etc.)
  • Grade 1-2: Fry first 100 words (about, been, could, each, etc.)
  • Timed mode: Word flashes for 3 seconds then hides — builds instant recognition
  • Based on the Dolch and Fry sight word lists used in schools
  • No data is saved or sent anywhere
