For Parents & Teachers

This phonics game helps children learn letter-sound relationships — a critical skill for early reading. Children see a letter or letter combination and choose the word that begins with that sound.

  • Easy: Single consonant sounds (B, C, D, etc.) with picture words
  • Medium: Vowel sounds (short and long A, E, I, O, U)
  • Hard: Consonant blends (BL, CR, ST) and digraphs (SH, CH, TH)
  • Visual emoji hints help pre-readers connect sounds to words
  • No data is saved or sent anywhere
