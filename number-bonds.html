For Parents & Teachers

Number bonds are a foundational concept for mental arithmetic. They show how numbers can be split into parts:

  • Easy: Bonds to 10 — the building blocks (e.g., 7 + ? = 10)
  • Medium: Bonds to 20 — bridging through 10
  • Hard: Bonds to 50 — more challenging splits
  • Visual circle layout helps children see the part-whole relationship
  • Multiple choice reduces frustration for younger learners
