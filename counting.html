For Parents & Teachers

Counting is one of the earliest math skills children develop. This game covers three key areas:

  • Count Objects (1-20): Builds one-to-one correspondence — the foundation of number sense
  • What Comes Next?: Reinforces number order and sequence recognition
  • Skip Counting: Counting by 2s, 5s, and 10s — a precursor to multiplication
  • Extra-large buttons designed for small fingers on tablets and phones
  • Multiple choice format keeps frustration low for young learners
