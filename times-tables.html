For Parents & Teachers

Speed challenges build fluency by encouraging rapid recall of multiplication facts:

  • Easy: 2×–5× tables (Grades 2-3)
  • Medium: 2×–9× tables (Grades 3-4)
  • Hard: 2×–12× tables (Grades 4-6)
  • 60-second timer creates urgency without being stressful
  • Wrong answers are shown briefly so children can learn from mistakes
  • Encourages beating their own score rather than competing with others
