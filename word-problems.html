For Parents & Teachers

This game builds reading comprehension and math problem-solving skills:

  • Easy: Single-step addition and subtraction within 20 (Grades K-2)
  • Medium: Addition/subtraction to 100 plus simple multiplication (Grades 2-3)
  • Hard: Multi-step problems with all four operations (Grades 3-5)
  • Problems are procedurally generated — every round is different!
  • Story contexts use familiar objects and names kids can relate to
  • Type-in answers build mental math and reading comprehension
